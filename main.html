Who I Am

  • Student
  • Brother
  • Teammate
  • Leader

I am the oldest of five kids in a large, Irish family of seven. Championing hard work and committment throughout my high school academic and athletic careers, I was the 2014 Valedictorian of an excellent public school, sporting a perfect GPA and a varsity football captain position.

The AP credits earned during my successful high school career enabled my current pursuit of two degrees in four years at Notre Dame, namely Electrical Engineering and Physics.

Now in my second year of school, I have conducted research with groups in both fields. My current leadership roles include acting as a Squad Lead for the NASA sponsored Notre Dame Rocketry Team and long-standing status as a campus-wide STEM tutor.

Outside of the classroom I enjoy playing the acoustic guitar, tinkering with Arch Linux, microcontrollers, and embedded systems, and playing dorm football and basketball.
